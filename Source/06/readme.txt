Code Example of Chapter 6, in HTML5 Game Development
--------------------------------------------------------

01. setup-the-game: Play button with sound effects.
02. game-bg: Setting the background styles and adding transition.
03. audio-playback-visualization: Playing the music with music dots moving.
04. taking-keyboard-inputs: Controlling the music lines with J, K, L keys.
05. adjust-volumes: Adjust the melody volume based on the player performance.
06. recording-level-data: Recording the music note data for the game.
07. supporting-touch-devices: Adding click and touch support to the game.
08. game over: Handling the melody ending event and show the success percentages.
